About Us

Best Christian Youth Group For Yourself and Your Family

Youth Alive Youth Alive is a Christ based and Catholic team that talks to youth about life and Christian values....
It is designed to build resilience among teens and young adults by equipping them to make healthy choices.

Mission

To empower Youth, mobilise communities and build futures.

Vision

Impacting biblical values to as many youth as possible in Uganda.

Services

Our Outstanding Services

Bible Study

In Christian communities, Bible study is the study of the Bible by people as a personal religious or spiritual practice. We believe people discover their true purpose and identity by knowing God through His Word. So we teach biblical knowledge and norms to the youth.

Giving Back

One of the most effective ways of giving back is volunteering your time at places like your local non-profit, school, homeless shelter, etc. Your time is valuable and gifting it can make a world of difference in the lives of many.

Evangelism

Here we dwell more at preaching the gospel with the intention of sharing the message and teachings of Jesus Christ not only to the youth, but to all people in our families and communities.


Voluntary Work

We believe that volunteering can help you make friends, learn new skills, advance your career, and even feel happier and healthier. Learn how to find the right volunteer opportunity for you.

Singing of Hymns

Because hymns are intended to reflect the qualities of God, they must have poetry that is beautiful, reverent, simple, accurate, and pure. We train the youth and kids how to sing and render vocal training skills to them.

Developing Talents

The talent that kids bear needs to be guided and assisted as it develops over time. In other words, the most efficient way to discover your kid's talents is to expose them to various activities so as to enable them to explore their space.
